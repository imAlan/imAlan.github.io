My 2018 Goals

"Without a goal you can't score"

Everyone is a work in progress. No one is perfect and everyone can improve. Having goals helps you progress towards that person you want to be. It shows that you are aware of yourself. It prevents you from getting too comfortable.

Last year I focused on creating a foundation of mental toughness and getting into physical shape. I got to travel to several places, run my first marathon, teach kids, focus less on other people's opinions...

This year I want to focus on consistency and learning.

Mental

  • Mediate daily
  • Disconnect, reflect and plan weekly
  • Read 10 books
  • Sleep earlier and wake up earlier

Physical

  • Run three marathons (NYC, CHI, SF)
  • Run a marathon under 4 hours
  • Join a running club
  • Stretch daily

Personal

  • Obtain my driver license
  • Improve my Chinese
  • Apply to graduate school
  • Teach Marley a new trick

Creative

  • Blog twice a month
  • Create and upload 5 videos
  • Redesign room
  • Take weekend trips outside of NYC
